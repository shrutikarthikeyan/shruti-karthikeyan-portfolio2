Cocoa Passion Artisanal Chocolates:

Crafting a seasonal packaging system, an e-commerce site and brand collateral for a luxury chocolate company, rooted in elegance and sensory detail.

Client:
Cynthia Chan,
Owner & Chocolatier
My Role:
Branding, Packaging,
Web Design, Social Media,
OOH/Ads
Timeline:
8 weeks, 2025
Live Site:
cocoapassion.ca

The Challenge

Create a complete brand identity for a first-time chocolatier launching her own artisanal storefront. The goal was to craft a visual language that felt premium, emotionally engaging, and scalable, while avoiding overly bold or trendy visuals that might distract from the brand’s quiet elegance.

The Solution

The packaging design is crafted with attention to detail, balancing visual richness with clarity, making each chocolate bar feel like a premium experience.

Seasonal Packaging

The brand identity captures the essence of luxury through refined typefaces, minimal layouts, and a color palette that evokes indulgence and sophistication.
