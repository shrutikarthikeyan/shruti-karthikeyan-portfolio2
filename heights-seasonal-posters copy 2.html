Heights Seasonal Posters

ILLUSTRATION | POSTER DESIGN

Project Overview

Client: Heights Merchants Association

Location: Burnaby, BC, Canada.

Scope: To develop a series of posters celebrating popular neighbourhood events such as Halloween on the Heights, Deck the Heights, and Lunar New Year. 

The Challenge

The Heights consists of over 350 businesses along Hastings Street. However, in recent years, these businesses have been losing customers to new shopping malls emerging around the district.

The Solution

I created posters that celebrate the unique community-focused spirit of the Heights. Each different event supported by local businesses reminds shoppers that the Heights’ is a different shopping experience, one that shopping malls cannot deliver.
