Natural Vitality Calm

WEBSITE DESIGN, ILLUSTRATION

Project Overview

Client: Natural Vitality Calm.
Location: Canada, US.
Scope: Develop sponsored content on a website promoting Natural Vitality’s Calm sleeping supplement. 

The Challenge

In today’s fast-paced world, people struggle with fatigue and stress. Natural Vitality Calm addresses this by offering products that promote relaxation and emotional balance. The target audience—women aged 20-39—value wellness and personal growth.
How can we make them feel that these products are approachable, safe and effective in calming their busy lives?

The Solution

I designed a website in which the illustration and design flow together to trace the emotional journey from anxiety to tranquility that Calm supports. Strategically placed CTAs with compelling copy guide users through this journey.​​​​​​​
