Natural Vitality Calm

WEBSITE DESIGN | ILLUSTRATION

Project Overview

Client: Natural Vitality Calm

Location: Canada, US.

Scope: Develop sponsored content on a website promoting Natural Vitality’s Calm sleeping supplement. 

The Challenge

People struggle with fatigue and stress in today’s fast-paced world. Natural Vitality Calm offers products that promote relaxation and emotional balance. Their target audience—women aged 20-39—value wellness and personal growth but are slow to trust such product safety and effectiveness.

The Solution

I designed a website in which the illustration and design flow together to trace the emotional journey from anxiety to tranquility that Calm supports. Strategically placed CTAs with compelling copy guide users through this journey.​​​​​​​

Illustrations

The illustrations embody a transformative process, capturing the shift in emotions from stress and anxiety to reflection and confidence, ultimately leading to a dream-like world and a balanced emotional state that Calm's supplements help achieve.​​​​​​​
