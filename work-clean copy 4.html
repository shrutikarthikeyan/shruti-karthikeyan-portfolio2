  • About Me

    Hello, I am a designer with strong illustration skills finishing up my last year at the IDEA School of Design at Capilano University in North Vancouver, BC.

    What I love most about design is seeing clients light up when I bring their brand to life. That is serious fun. Full disclosure: I love having all kinds of fun. I am a budding amusement park junkie, especially when I am at the top of the rollercoaster and I can see all the surrounding territory. It’s the same kind of thrill as when I discover a powerful branding concept and see where it can be applied.

    Otherwise I enjoy hiking, kayaking, traveling, watching movies and being quietly mischievous. (Last year I started a prank club at Capilano). Next year after I graduate, I will be ready to work so expect to have less amusement and pranking in my life.

    If you need any ideas for branding, illustration or pranking, shoot me an email!
